This guide will show new users how to interact with the Team Work app

Register

First things first lets register

Above is the register form that can be found by clicking the red register link from the main page. Once here it is as simple as filling the form out and creating a new account.Please note you can not add muliplte accounts with same details.

Menu

Ok lets have a look at our main menu

By clicking the hambuger in the top right corener (the three horizontal lines) a menu will appear. This the main menu simply select where you wish to go

Login

Ok lets login

Now enter your email and password you had registered with on the last step, once done press the blue login button

Availabilites

Ok lets set our availabilites

Select each day you can work by simply click yes or no on the days that you wish to work.Once done click the submit button below

Live Clock

Ok lets clock on

When you are ready to start work enter your clock on number that you registered with, don't worry if you forgotten it we can find where to see it in the next step. Once you have entered you correct clock on number press the green tick, if you have made a error press the red x to remove the number entered.

My Details

Lets have a look

Selecting the My Details from the menu tab will direct you to the my details part of the TeamWork app, here you will find 3 additional tabs
Details: this is a view of your current info
Update: this is where you can update your details
Settings: you can change theme or get help on how to use the app

Update My Details

Don't like something you see, lets chnage it

To arrive at this screen click the update tab in my details. Once here change update any details you wish to change and press submit

Settings

Select light or dark to change theme or come back here for more help by clicking the CLICK HERE

My Shifts

Ok see this is where you will see your current and past shifts. Simply click the day to see the shift details.
